Syllabus Units

Unit 1: Water Technology

Hardness of water, EDTA method, and water softening processes like Zeolite and Ion-exchange.

Unit 2: Instrumental Methods

Introduction to Spectroscopy, UV-Visible, and IR Spectroscopy fundamentals.

Unit 3: Engineering Materials

Polymers, Nanomaterials, and Composite materials used in modern industry.

Unit 4: Fuels and Combustion

Analysis of coal, Calorific value, and refining of Petroleum.

Unit 5: Corrosion Science

Mechanism of dry and wet corrosion, and methods for corrosion prevention.
